Our Story

Hi my name is Pat, and one day I got the awful idea of combining the sweetness of the cookie with the fishy umami flavor of salmon. After a series of crowdfunding that resulted in a whopping $62, I decided to ignore my wife's protests and quit the 9 to 5 to open my cookie shop. Though our specialty is the salmon cookie, we also have a variety of "less exotic" flavors my wife insisted we add to the menu. Be the first to taste our salmon flavored cookie so I can prove my wife wrong!
